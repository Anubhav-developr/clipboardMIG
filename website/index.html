ClipboardMIG

Real-time cross-device clipboard sync system using Android, Firebase Cloud Relay, and a Chrome Extension with automatic clipboard transfer and history management.

Download App Download Chrome Extension

How To Use?

  • Add your Firebase URL once, then keep the same room code on both your phone and PC.
  • Install the ClipboardMig Chrome Extension in Google Chrome on your computer.
  • Open the extension, choose Firebase Cloud, and tap Save & Connect.
  • Install the ClipboardMig app on your Android phone.
  • Paste the same Firebase URL and room code into the app.
  • Tap Start Capture so the app is ready to watch what you copy.
  • Copy any text or link on your phone and it will travel to your PC clipboard.
  • Paste it anywhere on your computer with Ctrl + V, just like normal.
  • Open the extension anytime to see your recent copied items.
  • Tap any saved item in the extension if you want to bring it back again.
